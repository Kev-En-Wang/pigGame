Rules of the game are:

1. Two dice are rolled. If neither is a one, their sum is added to the turn total.

2. If a single 1 is rolled, the active player scores nothing and ends this turn.

3. If two 1s are rolled, then the player loses all his score and ends this turn.

4. If the player ends his turn willingly, the turn total is added to his score.

5. First player to end a turn with 100 or more points wins.
